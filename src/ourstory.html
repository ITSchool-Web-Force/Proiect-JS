In 1953, on the glamorous and breathtaking Cote d'Azur near the city of Monaco, a small furniture design studio was founded with the goal of creating unique, high-end pieces that blended contemporary design with a sense of luxury and exclusivity. The founder, a young and visionary designer, aimed to create timeless furniture that would stand the test of time and be passed down for generations.

From the very beginning, the studio was dedicated to craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each piece was crafted by hand using only the finest materials, with a relentless pursuit of perfection. Traditional woodworking techniques and intricate details were employed to give each piece a sense of elegance and sophistication.

The studio quickly gained a reputation as one of the top furniture design houses in the world due to it's exclusive design and high quality materials. Its pieces were featured in the most exclusive homes, yachts, and jets of the world's elite, and were sought after by those who appreciate elegance, exclusivity, and high-end luxury.

In 1987, the company became a household name in the luxury furniture industry, opening showrooms and flagship stores in prestigious locations such as Milan, Paris, Munich, and New York. Today, the company is recognized as a true leader in the industry, and continues to create stunning, one-of-a-kind pieces that are crafted by hand. The company's commitment to craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a sense of sophistication remains the driving force behind the brand.
